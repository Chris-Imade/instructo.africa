Frequently Asked Questions

Instructo Africa is an organization dedicated to empowering African educators by providing affordable, accessible, and high-quality training tailored to the unique challenges and contexts of teaching in Africa. Our mission is to equip teachers with the skills and confidence needed for 21st-century classrooms.

Our training programs are specifically designed for African educators at all levels, including pre-service and in-service teachers, school leaders, and anyone involved in education across the continent who wants to enhance their skills and effectiveness.

We offer a wide range of courses covering Core Pedagogy, Technology Integration, Subject-Specific Strategies (STEM, Languages, Arts), Professional Development (Leadership, Entrepreneurship, Well-being), Culturally Responsive Teaching, and Future-Ready Skills like AI in education.

Our training is flexible! We offer programs online, offline, and through hybrid models to ensure accessibility for educators regardless of their location or internet connectivity.

Yes, affordability is a core principle. We strive to make our high-quality training accessible to as many African educators as possible. Contact us or check specific course details for pricing information.

Absolutely! Beyond training, we actively connect qualified teachers with recruitment opportunities at top schools, helping educators find fulfilling career paths.

Our courses are designed with the African context in mind. We incorporate culturally responsive teaching strategies and address real challenges faced in diverse African classrooms, including multilingual settings and resource constraints.

You can explore our courses on the website and use the 'Enroll Now' button, which will likely connect you via WhatsApp or another contact method. You can also reach out through our Contact page for specific inquiries.

We focus specifically on the needs of African educators, offering practical, culturally relevant training, recruitment support, and fostering a supportive community network to elevate the teaching profession across the continent.
